A series of limericks about CS

By Nathan Driggs

The first class is 142.

Where most learn the rumors are true

To do well in CS,

You must be fearless

And work 'til your fingers turn blue.

Number two is 235.

The ones whose dreams have survived.

One thing can be said:

Screw Iterat'r lab

But other than that, you'll be just fine.

For me, next was 224

Now, my sanity is no more.

It actually wasn't that bad...

Well, actually... yeah...

But still, 236 was worse.

236 was next in the mix,

It hit me like tons of bricks

Working hard is a must,

Barely got a C+

I almost failed 236.

240 is the next class,

But I was stronger after the last,

Made all the projects

But lest we forget,

I did well, and I did outlast.

Now, I am in 260

Before this, front end was a mystery.

I'm ready to work lots,

Make a few websites,

It doesn't seem too tricky.
