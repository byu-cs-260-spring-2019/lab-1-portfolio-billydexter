THE 90'S FESTIVAL

As a hobby, I sometimes work on a game I make in RPG maker called "VS THE WORLD". In the game, two of the main characters go to a town having a 90's festival, so I designed an entire world based on the decade of Hey Arnold and The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air. One of the enemies I designed was the Perm Whale, as shown below-

Perm Whale

Don't be disappointed if this pun is too perfect... It's already been done and it can't be taken back now.

There have been many other enemies, items, and references to the 90's that I've made in addition to the Perm Whale, including-

  • The Fresh Mints of Smell Fair
  • A Modern Pirate, a guy who illegally downloads DVD's and dresses up like a pirate
  • 3D Glasses
  • Roller Blades
  • Slow Internet, an enemy that attacks you by making you really frustrated
  • The 90's Jerks, which attack you by calling you names such as "Buttsnerd" and "Turddingus"
  • ...And many more!
